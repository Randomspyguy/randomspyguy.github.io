Hello! Welcome to my world. I am a wanna-be Electrical Engineer that has a dirty habbit of spending most of my time researching and developing code and learning about Computer Engineering. With the expence of college, most of what I know is self-taught. I started Uni in 2019 at Metropolitan State University of Denver, then moved online to Southern New Hampshire University due to the pandemic. Eventually having to take a forever 'Gap Year.'

I aspire to make my ideas come to life! I consume a lot of YouTube that involves the entire engineering process. A couple of my favorite YouTubers include: Michael Reeves, Wintergatan, bigclivedotcome, colinfurze, John Hammond, Kevin Powell, Mark Rober, Linus Tech Tips, Numberphile, Tech Ingredients, Veritasium, Vsauce, and SO many more! The reason I put emphasis on YouTube is becuase this is how I learn new things. Like mentioned above, college and uni are VERY expensive in the USA, and so some, like me, have to resort to self-teaching. This process is gruelling and very, very time consuming. But I find as you develop yourself in your skills at your own pace and understanding, it adds some sort of uniqueness to your work.
